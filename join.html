Values

We seek to develop an atmosphere in which every member feels welcome and respected. We are committed to building an open, diverse, inclusive, and accepting environment in which talented individuals from all backgrounds and identities can learn, grow, and develop. Everyone is expected to treat others with respect and courtesy, irrespective of such factor as (but not limited to) race, religion, sexual orientation, gender, disability, age, or national origin.
